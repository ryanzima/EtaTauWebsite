About Eta Tau

Who we are

Eta Tau is a professional business club who strives to prepare students for the commercial world. We focus on preparing our members to excel through leadership training, networking experiences, as well as professional development.

Beyond career preparation, we emphasize the importance of building a strong, diverse, and supportive community. As one, we aim to prepare the next generation of business professionals.

Our Goal

To prepare the next generation of business professionals for the commercial world.
